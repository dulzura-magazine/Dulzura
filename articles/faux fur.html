Faux Fur: The Ultimate Statement

By Elisa Hernandez Reyes

Faux fur is the ultimate shortcut to looking chic; it’s everywhere—and I’m sure you’ve seen it. From your favorite influencer and designer’s show to the girl you pass on the street whose closet you wish you could shop, faux fur has become a staple in high-fashion winter wardrobes. When you think of fur, you probably think of exaggerated coats. These may seem intimidating when you’ve never styled one before, but they’re easier to wear than you may think. Fur has evolved from the coat and has been adopted into every aspect of the wardrobe. Now, fur can be seen on shoes, skirts, accessories, and so much more, making it the ultimate shortcut to your fashion-forward winter wardrobe.

Shapes that Matter

In the Fall 2025 runways, there were key elements that were cohesive throughout. The most notable shows included Miu Miu, Fendi, and Khaite, all bringing different variations and styling to their runways. The most significant styles included maxi, oversized, and cropped coats, along with an array of accessories such as fur stoles. To accompany them, popular color choices like brown, burgundy, and emerald (to make a statement) set the bar for the fur theme of this fall-winter season.

7 Ways to Wear It Now

Elevated Athleisure

The best way to cheat your way to chic is to pair your fur with your daily essentials. Start with an oversized maxi coat in any neutral. Then, pick out your favorite gray sweat set and finish with sneakers or heels, whichever you prefer. Watch as the contrast of the coat with your sweats transforms into a high-fashion statement, a look often seen on Elsa Hosk.

Maximum Minimalism

While accessories and maximalism are rising in popularity, there are some who prefer the more minimalistic approach. Yes, your coat can be the only accessory. Start with a mid to maxi coat in a bright color. Then, it’s your choice of a slip dress or mini skirt, paired with sheer tights. Finally, either pumps or knee-high boots will complete the look. By eliminating anything that makes the outfit feel busy or cluttered, the focus is drawn back onto the coat.

Proportion Play

If you are interested in a more casual look, this next look is a great option. Start with a cropped fur jacket in a texture of your choice. Add wide-leg trousers or barrel-leg jeans and pair with ankle boots or flats. This balanced look defines the waistline and prevents the overall shapelessness that tends to happen with longer coats.

Monochromatic Moment

Faux fur allows you to create depth with its texture, even within a single color. Start with a coat in any color of choice, then pair it with a matching knit sweater and trousers in the same tone. Finally, complete the color scheme with matching loafers or boots. The monochromatic palette is enhanced by the texture of the coat and creates a timeless, quiet luxury quality.

Practical Print

The best way to incorporate any print into your wardrobe successfully is to treat it like a staple rather than a statement. Take a printed faux fur coat and layer it with a simple white button-down or turtleneck. Pair with blue jeans and ankle boots or flats for a timeless, cool, and chic look.

Strategic Softness

For some, a coat might feel like too much. In that case, try faux fur on a smaller scale. Pair a blazer with a faux fur stole or scarf, or choose a faux fur bag or hat to add subtle sophistication without the full commitment.

Tailored Trim

Another way to incorporate fur without making it the centerpiece is to use it as an accent. Choose a trench coat or leather jacket with a faux fur trim and style it with denim and loafers. This creates a vintage look that feels cozy while still providing structure.

Worth the Investment?

A faux fur coat may seem like a big investment, and it is, but many brands offer accessible prices while maintaining good quality. These include H&M, Aritzia, Banana Republic, Mango, and GAP. Designers such as Stella McCartney and Miu Miu also offer faux fur options. Ultimately, you should invest in what fits your style and needs best.

This season, faux fur is sure to be a staple. Whether you choose to embrace it and all the drama that follows or maximize the minimalism, you are well on your way to your dream wardrobe and taking the ultimate shortcut to chic this winter.
