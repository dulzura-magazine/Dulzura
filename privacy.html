privacy policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Dulzura respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit our website or join our email list.

information we collect

Email list: If you subscribe, we collect the name and email address you submit. We may also store a timestamp (the date/time you joined).

Basic analytics: Like most websites, we may receive limited technical information (such as browser type, device type, or approximate location) through standard hosting/analytics tools. We use this only to understand site performance.

how we use your information

We use subscriber information to send Dulzura emails (issues, updates, and occasional announcements). We do not sell your personal information.

images and editorial content

Some images featured on Dulzura are used for editorial and inspirational purposes. Unless otherwise stated, Dulzura does not claim ownership of third-party images and credits creators when available. If you believe any content should be removed or credited differently, please contact us.

sharing

We do not sell your information. We may use trusted service providers (for example, email delivery or form processing) solely to operate the site and deliver communications.

your choices

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails. You can also request removal by contacting us.

data retention

We keep subscriber information as long as you remain subscribed or until you request removal.

contact

Questions? Email us at dulzuramagazine@gmail.com..
